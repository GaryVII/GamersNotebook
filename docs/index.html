What is Gamers Notebook?

Gamers Notebook is a website created by me, Garrett Castoldi, to share my knowledge on video games and other related topics. On this website I will go over; My favorite games that I have played on the Game Rankings page, I will talk about Halo and how influential the game has been for me and many of the other games to come after it on the Halo page, I will discuss Call of Duty and how it redefined multiplayer games for me on the Call of Duty page, I will also discuss battle royal and how this sub genre of games has taken over the market as of late on the Battle Royale page, I will talk about the pc and console communities and their differences on the PC vs Console page, I will talk about some of the latest technologies and my opinions on them on the Tech, and lastly I will have a Form for you fill out to suggest some topics to add to my website.

Why?

The purpose of this website is for me to share my opinions and my ideas with the public in hopes of sparking discussion. I hope for this better my knowledge of video games and tech and I hope for it to be seen as a site that gives the real truths about some of these games and the technologies. In the end I hope for this site to introduce others to my favorite games and technologies and for others to tell me about their favorites in the Form.
